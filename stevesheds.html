Steve's Sheds

Steve's Sheds is a premium garden shed company launching in the Irish market. With no existing customer base or website, we were brought in to design a full digital presence from scratch — one that could build trust, communicate quality, and convert first-time visitors into quote requests.

With no existing customers to interview, we ran a two-phase research strategy: a targeted survey of 15 participants across a broad age range, followed by analysis of Irish consumer forums like Boards.ie and AskAboutMoney. A consistent pattern emerged users distrust builders who leave jobs unfinished and are willing to pay a premium for reliability. These insights shaped the core value proposition: One Price, One Person full installation, full site cleanup, no surprises.

We designed iteratively across three fidelity levels: paper wireframes, medium-fidelity Figma wireframes, and a high-fidelity interactive prototype. The site structure was validated through card sorting and tree testing before any visual design began, ensuring navigation matched real user expectations rather than assumptions.

Three rounds of usability testing informed each design iteration, confirming that users responded to a visual-first layout while flagging areas where trust signals needed strengthening. The final design system covering colour, typography, spacing, and accessibility standards ensured consistency across all screens and a clear foundation for future content updates.

The outcome: A complete high-fidelity website covering 30+ screens across desktop and mobile in Figma. The design prioritises trust, clarity and ease of use making it simple for customers to find the right shed and take the next step.
