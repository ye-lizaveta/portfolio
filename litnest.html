What is LitNest?

LitNest is a mobile book tracking and social reading app. The name combines "Literary" and "Nest" a cosy place for readers to organise, track and share their reading lives. The project was driven by real user research into how reading enthusiasts track and share their habits.

The Problem: Reading is often a solitary activity. Existing platforms like Goodreads feel outdated and don't encourage real social interaction between readers. LitNest was designed to bridge that gap making reading feel connected, social and personal.

Solution: LitNest reimagines the reading app experience with a focus on intuitive navigation, visual consistency and genuine social connection. Every feature was designed to be immediately discoverable, with a centralised navigation bar, clear system feedback and a warm, minimal aesthetic that feels personal rather than clinical.

Key features

  • Buddy Reads with spoiler-preventing page-specific comments
  • Community feed showing what friends are reading, lending and rating.
  • Local book exchange trade books within your area.
  • Reading challenges with progress tracking.
  • Direct messaging and community interaction.
