What is Steve's Sheds?

Steve's Sheds is a fully responsive website designed for a real garden shed business based in Limerick, Ireland. The project was completed as part of my UX/UI Design module at the University of Limerick, designing a complete end-to-end web experience across desktop and mobile from the homepage through to product browsing, checkout and contact.

The Problem: Steve Guerin is an experienced tradesman with 45 years in construction, a Sunday Tribune column and regular appearances on RTÉ and Limerick's Live 95FM. Despite his credibility and expertise, his online presence didn't reflect the quality of his service. The brief was to design a website that builds trust, communicates his process clearly and makes it easy for customers to browse sheds and request a quote.

Solution: A warm, nature-inspired design using deep greens and earthy tones that reflect the outdoor, craftsmanship feel of the brand. The site guides users through a clear journey understanding the service, browsing shed options by size, following Steve's 4-step process, reading customer testimonials and getting in touch.

Key pages designed

  • Homepage with hero, service overview and testimonials.
  • Sheds catalogue small, medium and large with filtering.
  • Process page 4 clear steps from consultation to clean-up.
  • Individual shed pages with specs and options.
  • Shopping basket and checkout flow.
  • Contact and quote request form.
  • Guides blog section.
  • About page.
  • Full mobile responsive versions of all pages.

The outcome: A complete high-fidelity website covering 30+ screens across desktop and mobile in Figma. The design prioritises trust, clarity and ease of use making it simple for customers to find the right shed and take the next step.

Tools: Figma
